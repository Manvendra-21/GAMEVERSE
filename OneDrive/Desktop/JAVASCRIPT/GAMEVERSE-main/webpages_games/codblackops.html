Story :

Call of Duty: Black Ops II (2012) is a first-person shooter game by Treyarch, set in a fictionalized version of the 1960s Cold War era. It follows the story of Alex Mason and his team as they navigate the complexities of the Cold War while facing off against Soviet forces and a mysterious antagonist named Raul Menendez. The game features a branching storyline with multiple endings based on player choices.
