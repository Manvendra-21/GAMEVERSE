Story :

Red Dead Redemption 2 (2018) is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game by Rockstar Games, acting as a prequel to the 2010 original. Set in 1899 during the decline of the Wild West, it follows outlaw Arthur Morgan as he navigates the collapse of the Van der Linde gang while facing internal conflicts, government forces, and his own morality.
