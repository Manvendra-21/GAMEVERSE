Story :

God of War Ragnarök follows Kratos and a teenage Atreus trying to prevent the prophesied apocalypse while navigating Fimbulwinter, three years after the previous game. Atreus seeks his destiny as Loki to stop Odin, while Kratos battles his past to protect his son. They unite the realms to fight Asgard, ultimately destroying Odin and defying fate to choose a new path.
